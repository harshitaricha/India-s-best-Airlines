4.GoAir


GoAir has secured the position of number four in the list of Best Airlines in India. The airline was founded in Nov 2005 by Indian industrialist, Jeh Wadia. The airline is a wholly-owned subsidiary of the Wadia Group. GoAir has started its operations in November 2005. In October 2017 the airline has the fifth largest airline in India and has 8.4% of market share. Advertisement GoAir has operated a fleet of Airbus A320 aircraft configured as all economies. As of September 2019, the airline operates approx 230 daily flights to 30 major destinations, which include 24 domestic and 6 international sectors. The airline has its hubs at Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Kolkata, and Kannur.
