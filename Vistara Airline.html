5.Vistara Airline


Vistara secured its position in the top five with the rank of number five in the best airlines in India. The airline was founded in 2013 as a joint venture between Tata Sons and Singapore Airlines, Tata SIA Airlines Limited. Vistara is operated as an Indian full-service carrier and having its headquarters in Gurgaon. The airlines have started its operation from 9 January 2015 with its inaugural flight between Delhi and Mumbai. The airline had carried more than 20 Lacks passengers by June 2016 and has a 4.7% share of the domestic carrier market as of May 2019.
